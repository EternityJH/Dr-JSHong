Research Interests

My research interests lie at the intersection of medical imaging, artificial intelligence, and clinical translation. I focus on the development and validation of AI algorithms for automated detection, segmentation, and quantification of brain tumors in MRI, particularly longitudinal tumor tracking and integration into radiology workflow through DICOM-compatible outputs.

My work extends to real-world clinical integration, including the design and deployment of AI-assisted diagnostic systems within hospital environments, such as backend model orchestration, real-time inference, and user interface development with an emphasis on regulatory compliance. Ultimately, my goal is to bridge technical innovation and clinical applicability to improve patient care.
